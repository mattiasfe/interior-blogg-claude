The space under your bed is often wasted — storage beds turn it into some of the most useful real estate in your home.

## Drawer-based storage beds

Built-in drawers on one or both sides of the bed frame are easy to access and great for clothing, bedding or shoes.

## Lift-up storage beds

A hydraulic or gas-lift mechanism raises the entire mattress platform, revealing a large storage compartment underneath — perfect for bulky items like suitcases.

## Open-frame beds with bins

A simple raised bed frame leaves room for storage bins or baskets underneath, which is a budget-friendly option.

## What to consider before buying

- **Mattress access**: lift-up beds require you to clear the bed before opening
- **Floor protection**: drawers on casters can scratch floors over time — look for felt pads
- **Weight**: storage beds are heavier, so plan for help when moving

If you're tight on closet space, a storage bed can effectively give you an extra small room's worth of storage — without taking up any additional floor space.
