A small balcony is still outdoor space — and with a few smart choices, it can become your favorite spot in the apartment.

## Choose foldable furniture

A small foldable table and chairs can be tucked away when not in use, freeing up space for plants or storage.

## Go vertical with plants

Wall-mounted planters, hanging baskets and tiered plant stands let you fill your balcony with greenery without crowding the floor.

## Add soft lighting

String lights or a small solar lantern make even the smallest balcony feel like an evening retreat.

## Use a weatherproof rug

A rug instantly makes a concrete balcony feel like an extension of your living room.

## Add a privacy screen

A simple bamboo screen or trellis with climbing plants creates privacy from neighbors without blocking light.

## Keep a consistent color scheme

Tying your balcony's colors to your indoor decor makes the space feel like a natural continuation of your home.

Even five square meters can become a peaceful little escape with the right setup.
