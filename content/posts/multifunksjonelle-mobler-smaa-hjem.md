The right furniture can be the difference between a cramped apartment and a home that feels spacious and functional. Here are our favorites within multifunctional furniture.

## Stylish sofa beds

A good sofa bed works as a seating area during the day and a guest bed at night — perfect for studio apartments.

## Extendable dining tables

A small dining table that can be expanded when needed gives you flexibility without taking up unnecessary space in everyday life.

## Ottomans with storage

An ottoman with room for blankets, cushions or seasonal clothing is discreet, stylish and incredibly practical.

## Bed frames with drawers

Make use of the space under the bed for clothes, bedding or other items you don't use daily.

## Wall-mounted desks

A foldable desk can be flipped up when you need a home office, and folded back down when you need the space for something else.

With thoughtful furniture choices, you can create a home that adapts to your life — not the other way around.
