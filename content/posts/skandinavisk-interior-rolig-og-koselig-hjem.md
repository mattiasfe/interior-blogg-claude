Scandinavian interior design has conquered the world with its simple elegance. The philosophy is built on functionality, natural materials and a calm color palette that creates a warm and welcoming home.

## Neutral colors as a base

White, beige, light gray and muted greens form the foundation of Scandinavian style. These colors reflect light and give the room a sense of calm.

## Natural materials

Wood, linen, wool and rattan are recurring elements in Scandinavian homes. These materials add texture and warmth, and age beautifully over time.

## Functional minimalism

Every piece of furniture and every object should have a purpose. Avoid unnecessary clutter — let the room breathe, and let a few thoughtful details shine.

## Hygge through lighting

Soft, warm lighting from candles, floor lamps and pendant lights creates the cozy atmosphere Scandinavians are known for.

## Plants and green touches

A couple of green plants bring life and freshness into an otherwise neutral room, connecting the interior to the nature outside.

The result is a home that feels timeless, calm and deeply personal — no matter the size of your living space.
