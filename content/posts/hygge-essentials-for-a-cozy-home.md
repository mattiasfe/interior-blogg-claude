Hygge isn't a style — it's a feeling. But a few well-chosen items can help you create that feeling at home, no matter your budget.

## 1. Chunky knit throws

Drape one over the sofa or the end of your bed for instant warmth and texture.

## 2. Candles, candles, candles

Unscented soy candles in simple holders create soft, flickering light that no overhead bulb can match.

## 3. Sheepskin or wool rugs

A small rug by the bed or in front of the sofa adds softness underfoot.

## 4. Ceramic mugs

Handmade or hand-thrown mugs make your morning coffee feel like a small ritual.

## 5. Linen bedding

Slightly rumpled, breathable linen feels relaxed and lived-in in the best way.

## 6. Books you actually want to read

A small stack on the nightstand or coffee table invites you to slow down.

## 7. Dimmable lighting

Swap harsh overhead lights for warm, dimmable lamps in the evening.

## 8. Greenery

A few simple potted plants bring life and softness to any room.

Start with one or two of these, and you'll already feel the difference.
