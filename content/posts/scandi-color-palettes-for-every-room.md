Color is one of the easiest ways to bring Scandinavian style into your home. The trick is choosing soft, muted tones that work together rather than fighting for attention.

## Living room: warm whites and wood

Start with a warm white or off-white wall color, then layer in light wood furniture and woven textiles for warmth.

## Bedroom: dusty blues and greens

Soft blues and sage greens create a restful atmosphere — perfect paired with crisp white linens and a wool rug.

## Kitchen: stone and sand

Stone gray cabinetry with sand-colored countertops feels timeless and pairs beautifully with brass or matte black hardware.

## Bathroom: pale gray and white

Keep bathrooms airy with pale gray tiles, white fixtures and a single green plant for a touch of life.

A cohesive palette across your home creates a sense of calm flow from room to room — the heart of Scandinavian living.
